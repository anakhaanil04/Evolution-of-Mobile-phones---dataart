Evolution of Computer

the years and timeline

Timeline

Motorola DynaTAC 8000X

1983

Weighed nearly 1 kilogram, it offered 30 minutes of talk time and required 10 hours to charge.

Motorola StarTAC

1996

Introduced the flip phone design, SMS, improving portability and style.

Feature Phone Era

2000-2006

Nokia, Sony Ericsson, and Motorola dominated the market, models like the Nokia 3310 became cultural phenomena.

Birth of the Smartphone

2007-2010

Apple's iPhone revolutionized the industry with a multi-touch capacitive screen, removing the need for physical keypads. Android OS introduced an open-source alternative, enabling wider smartphone adoption.
